---\nname: VisionController\ndescription: Advanced window management and vision capture tool for AI agents.\n---\n\n# VisionController\n\nProvides precise window control, focused capturing, and coordinate mapping.\n\n## Commands\n\n### 1. Focus & Capture Window\n```bash\npython window_manager.py capture --title \"Chrome\" --output \"capture.png\"\n```\nReturns: Path to image, Window Screen Coordinates (X, Y, W, H)\n\n### 2. Capture Region\n```bash\npython window_manager.py capture --region \"0,0,1920,1080\" --output \"full.png\"\n```\n\n### 3. Click Relative to Window\n```bash\npython window_manager.py click --title \"Chrome\" --x 100 --y 200\n```\n(Clicks at Window_X + 100, Window_Y + 200)\n
